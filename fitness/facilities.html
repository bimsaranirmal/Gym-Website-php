The Therapies.

ICE BATH

A cold shower might seem appealing after a sweaty day in Sri Lanka, but our ice bath will take your recovery to the next level. Cold water immersion can accelerate your recovery and prevent muscular swelling after an intense workout. Ask us about ice bath sessions supervised by our expert trainers.

SPORTS MASSAGE

Take a bumpy tuk-tuk ride and your body will receive a backseat bruising. Looking for more effective pressure therapy? We have specialists on hand to offer deep-tissue sports massages. Whether you have a knot that needs kneading or want full-body relief, speak to the Makahiya team to arrange a massage in our dedicated therapy room.
