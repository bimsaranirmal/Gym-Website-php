Cardio Classes

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, focuses on increasing heart rate and improving endurance. These classes often include high-energy, rhythmic movements designed to boost calorie burn, enhance stamina, and improve heart health.

Benefits:

  • Burns calories and fat
  • Improves heart and lung capacity
  • Enhances endurance and stamina

Strength Training Classes

Strength training focuses on building muscle, improving tone, and increasing overall strength. It typically involves resistance exercises using body weight, free weights, or resistance bands.

Benefits:

  • Increases muscle mass and strength
  • Enhances metabolism and calorie burn
  • Improves bone density and joint health
  • Boosts overall functional fitness

Yoga Classes

Yoga combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to improve flexibility, strength, and mental clarity. It’s ideal for enhancing balance, reducing stress, and increasing mindfulness.

Benefits:

  • Improves flexibility and mobility
  • Builds core strength and balance
  • Promotes mental relaxation and reduces anxiety
  • Enhances overall body alignment and posture

Flexibility and Mobility Classes

Flexibility and mobility classes focus on improving range of motion, joint health, and muscle elasticity. These classes are designed to complement other forms of exercise, reduce injury risk, and enhance overall movement patterns.

Benefits:

  • Increases range of motion in joints and muscles
  • Reduces the risk of injury and muscle strain
  • Improves posture and body alignment
  • Enhances performance in other workouts and daily activities
